Just Above Midtown: Changing Spaces
Last Look Feb 19–20
This is the final weekend to experience Just Above Midtown: Changing Spaces, an exhibition about the experimental space, led by Linda Goode Bryant, where Black art flourished in the 1970s and ‘80s.

Discover the energy and ethos that came together at this path-making gallery, and explore works by David Hammons, Lawrence D. “Butch” Morris, Senga Nengudi, Lorraine O’Grady, Howardena Pindell, Randy Williams, and many more.

As a member, you enjoy exclusive access to Just Above Midtown: Changing Spaces, named Best Art of 2022 by the New York Times, for two full days after it closes to the public.
